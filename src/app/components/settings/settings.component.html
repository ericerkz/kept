CalDAV Sync @if (hasExistingCaldav) { }

Push reminders to iCloud, Fastmail, Nextcloud, ownCloud, or any CalDAV-compatible calendar server.

@if (showInstructions === 'caldav') {
  1. Find your CalDAV calendar URL for your provider:
    • Apple iCloud: https://caldav.icloud.com/ — use an app-specific password from appleid.apple.com
    • Fastmail: https://caldav.fastmail.com/dav/principals/user/you@fastmail.com/
    • Nextcloud: https://your-domain.com/remote.php/dav/calendars/username/personal/
    • ownCloud: https://your-domain.com/remote.php/dav/calendars/username/personal/
    • Self-hosted (Radicale, Baikal): consult your server docs — often /cal/ or /dav/
  2. Enter your username — usually your email address.
  3. Enter an app password (not your main account password). Most providers require this; generate one in your account's security settings.
  4. Click Test connection to verify, then Save.
}
